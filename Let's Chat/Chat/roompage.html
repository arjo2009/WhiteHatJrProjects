BOLD
Want to highlight something?... Then use the Bold your text by clicking on the bold button. This makes all the text in the typing box bolder.Any text further written will become bold. Even after clearing the typing area the text written will be bold. If you want to turn of the bold then double click on the bold button.

COLOR
Click on the black square and choose your color. Then click on the crayon to make the text color to the color chosen. They placeholder of the typing area may look gray but any text typed after changing the color will be the chosen color.There is no back button so to tuen it back to black you have to change the color manually
